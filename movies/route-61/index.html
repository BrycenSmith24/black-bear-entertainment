Route 61

The wrong man. The wrong town. The wrong time.

Synopsis

About the film

After taking a remote highway route, Ethan Cole arrives in a town that feels disconnected from the world around it. At first it seems like a simple detour, but the more time he spends there, the more he realizes that Route 61 is tied to something far darker than a road.

Strange events, unfamiliar faces, and a growing sense that the town is watching him force Ethan to question what is real and what is not. Every choice pushes him deeper into the mystery.
